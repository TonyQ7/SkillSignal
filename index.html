The Problem

Early-career hiring runs on weak proxies

Degrees and CVs are poor indicators of real capability. AI makes applications easier to generate, not easier to trust. Employers need evidence, not more keywords.

"Skill gaps are the single biggest barrier to business transformation, and current hiring infrastructure still does a poor job of making actual capability visible." World Economic Forum, 2025
📄
CV theatre. Polished documents that reveal nothing about real capability or problem-solving approach.
🤖
AI-generated noise. Every candidate now has a perfect cover letter. The signal-to-noise ratio is collapsing.
🎓
Pedigree bias. Hiring defaults to university brand and GPA — missing high-capability candidates from non-traditional paths.
🔍
Screening overload. Recruiters spend hours reviewing hundreds of nearly identical applications for a single junior role.
